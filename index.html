About the Event

HackHPC@ADMI26 is a five-day virtual hackathon built around structured collaboration, mentoring, and advanced computing problem-solving. Each morning session launches a focused theme — from kickoff and team formation to project plan reviews, mentorship demos, advanced workshops, and final preparation — while afternoon check-ins keep teams on track with progress updates and troubleshooting support.

Throughout the week, teams will develop project plans, present regular progress updates, refine technology choices, and practice their pitches before delivering final presentations and celebrating achievements at the closing awards session. Expect a mix of hands-on project work, iterative feedback, sponsor engagement, and a strong emphasis on teamwork and real-world HPC application.

  • Who: Students, researchers, and early career technologists forming teams to build HPC and AI-enabled science gateway solutions.
  • What: A structured, virtual hackathon with kickoff sessions, project planning, mentor check-ins, technical workshops, dry-run presentations, and a final awards showcase.
  • When: June 22–26, 2026, with morning and afternoon sessions each day to keep momentum and provide support.
  • Where: Fully virtual — join via Zoom and collaborate online using your team workspace and shared tools.
